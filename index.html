About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate of Software Engineering Institute, East China Normal University (or ECNU), supervised by Prof. Ting Su. Currently, I'm a visiting scholar in AST Lab, ETH Zurich, supervised by Zhendong Su. Previously, I obtained my B.S. degree at ECNU (2019-2023).

Research

My research interests include include compiler validation and engineering. Currently, I'm exploring advanced compiler optimization techniques for training and deploying deep neural network programs.
